## Template Guidelines

### Hook (First 2 Lines)
- Must stop the scroll
- Create tension or curiosity
- Avoid clichés like "I'm excited to announce..."

### Body Structure Options

**Option A: Contrarian Take**
```
The conventional wisdom says X.
But here's what nobody talks about: Y.
The real issue is Z.
```

**Option B: Before/After**
```
Before: [pain state]
After: [transformed state]
The shift: [what changed]
```

**Option C: Data-Driven**
```
[Surprising statistic]
Why this matters: [context]
What to do about it: [action]
```

### CTA Options
- Ask a thought-provoking question
- Invite discussion: "What's your experience?"
- Soft product mention: "We're working on this at [company]"

### Formatting
- Use line breaks every 2-3 lines
- Keep paragraphs to 1-3 sentences
- Use bullet points sparingly
- Target 1,300-2,000 characters

### Optimal Posting
- Tuesday-Thursday, 8-10 AM local time
- Engage with comments in first hour
- No external links in first hour
