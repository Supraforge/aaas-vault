# [BRAND_NAME] Voice & Tone Guidelines

## Brand Personality
*   **Authoritative (9/10)**: We are the experts in ASPICE, ISO 26262, and Engineering Quality. We don't guess; we reason.
*   **Provocative but Grounded (7/10)**: We challenge "natural laws" of bureaucracy (e.g. "Three months of audit prep is a systemic failure").
*   **Efficient (10/10)**: Time is our main currency. Everything we do is about returning engineering hours to innovation.
*   **Direct & Transparent**: No fluff. No "AI magic." Just "Neural Quality Layer" logic.

## Content Strategy Principles
1.  **The 90-Minute Anchor**: Always reference the "90-minute audit baseline" as the gold standard of speed vs. manual prep.
2.  **Problem-First Agitation**: Lead with the "40% engineering time waste" or "$50M frozen program" pain points.
3.  **Cross-Tool Logic**: Emphasize that we run **on top** of existing tools (Codebeamer, Jira, Polarion).
4.  **Neuro-Symbolic Framing**: Explain AI reliability through the lens of logic and verification, not just probabilistic generation.
