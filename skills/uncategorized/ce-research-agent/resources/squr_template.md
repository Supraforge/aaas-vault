# SQUR Contextual Framework Document

This document serves as a high-context reference framework for the SQUR project, summarizing key research insights and providing strategic context to guide product development, marketing, AI prompting, and lead generation initiatives.

## 🧠 Company Overview
- **Name:** SQUR (pronounced "secure")
- **Offering:** AI-powered, autonomous penetration testing platform
- **Mission:** Democratize cybersecurity by replacing manual, slow, and expensive pentests with continuous, fast, and cost-effective autonomous tests
- **Key Promise:** Full pentest completed within 24 hours, zero security expertise required to initiate

## 🔐 Product Highlights
- **Fully Autonomous Pentesting:** Covers reconnaissance, vulnerability discovery, exploitation, validation
- **One-Click Setup:** Point-and-click simplicity; no in-house security expertise needed
- **Real-Time Dashboard:** Vulnerabilities surfaced live; comprehensive audit-ready reports post-test
- **Continuous Testing:** Supports CTEM (Continuous Threat Exposure Management) practices
- **Verified Findings:** Exploits vulnerabilities to confirm validity, drastically reducing false positives
- **Compliance Reporting:** Auto-generates reports aligned with ISO 27001, SOC 2, EU CRA, NIS2
- **Pricing:** Transparent (~€1,995 per test); bundles and retests available

## 👥 Target Audience
- CTOs, CISOs, CDOs, security engineers, developers, ethical hackers, compliance officers
- Mid-market and enterprise companies, especially in highly regulated industries (e.g., fintech, SaaS, healthcare)

## 🌍 Market Focus
- **Primary Region:** DACH (Germany, Austria, Switzerland)
- **Secondary:** Broader EU, UK, North America

## 🧠 AI & Governance
- **Core Strength:** Trustworthy autonomous AI pentester with governance policies baked into decision-making
- **Collaboration:** KASTEL Labs (Karlsruhe Institute of Technology) for AI governance & compliance
- **Explainability:** Actions and decisions of AI logged, audited, and explained contextually
- **Safety Controls:** Operates strictly within scope and sandboxing rules

## ⚔️ Competitive Landscape
**Primary Competitors:**
- **Horizon3.ai (NodeZero):** Broad autonomous testing (network/cloud); strong in US market
- **Pentera:** Security validation platform; more enterprise-focused
- **XBOW, Penligent, Terra Security:** AI pentesting startups with varied approaches (fully automated vs hybrid)
- **Cobalt, Synack:** PTaaS platforms with human-led testing models
- **Other Adjacent Tools:** BAS (e.g., Cymulate), AI scanners (ImmuniWeb), PentestGPT (human assistant)

**SQUR Differentiators:**
- Instant 24h test start, no human coordination
- Transparent pricing
- In-platform retesting and remediation validation
- Focus on explainable, regulated AI (academic collaboration)
- Emphasis on low false positive rate through in-test validation

## 📈 Strategic Positioning
- **Messaging Pillars:**
