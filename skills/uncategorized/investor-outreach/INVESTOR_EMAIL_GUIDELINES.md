# 📄 Investor Email Guidelines

This document defines the rules for generating and sending outreach emails to venture capitalists and strategic partners.

## 🎯 Strategic Objective

Move investors from "Cold" to "Interacting with the Avatar" as the primary conversion event. The secondary goal is a 15-minute discovery call.

## 👥 The Investor Persona

Based on the `SPLIT PERSONA` document:

- **Tone**: Analytical, Calm, Direct.
- **Stance**: "If this is big, it's because it changes when risk becomes visible."
- **Focus**: Why-now, Wedge sharpness (VW/Audi verification), and Failure modes.
- **Avoid**: Feature depth, generic "AI hype", and implementation details.

## 📝 Structure & Formatting

1. **The Hook**: Must lead with either **Logos (VW/Audi)** or the **Autonomous Proxy**.
2. **Body**:
   - Explicitly mention the **€190k Pre-Seed round** and the **strategic technology partner**.
   - Use **bolding** sparingly but strategically for metrics and urgency.
3. **The Link**: The primary CTA must be the [Interactive Deck](https://www.supra-forge.com/pitch-deck.html).
4. **Signature**:

   ```
   Best,
   
   Tobias Müller &
   Jorian Bos
   
   CEO & CRO | SUPRAFORGE
   ```

## 🧪 A/B Testing Strategy

- **Template 1 (Traction)**: Best for traditional VCs who look for social proof and big logos.
- **Template 2 (Tech-Forward)**: Best for DeepTech or AI-first funds who value a high-tech "WOW" factor.
- **Template 3 (Problem/Solution)**: Best for industrial-focused investors who understand the "compliance tax" pain points.

## 🚫 Anti-Patterns (Do NOT use)

- "Building the next unicorn" (Too hyped)
- "Disrupting the market" (Too generic)
- "Revolutionary AI" (Use "Intelligence Layer" or "Quality Gateway" instead)
- "Hoping to connect" (Be more direct: "Do you have 15 minutes?")
