## 🎨 Brand Voice Quick Guide

### Tone Attributes

1. **Engineering-Authoritative** - Speak from technical expertise
2. **Provocative** - Challenge assumptions, create tension
3. **ROI-Focused** - Quantify everything (40%, 3 months, 10x)
4. **Authentic** - Share real experiences

### Key Lexicon

| Instead of... | Use... |
|--------------|--------|
| Problem | **Systemic failure** |
| AI tool | **Neural quality layer** |
| Delayed feedback | **Late visibility** |
| Traceability | **Golden thread** |
| Automated output | **By-product of development** |
| Fast compliance | **Compliance at the speed of code** |

### Voice Checklist

Before posting, verify:

- [ ] Uses 2+ key lexicon terms
- [ ] Includes specific metrics
- [ ] Challenges an assumption
- [ ] Demonstrates technical authority
- [ ] Ties to business/ROI impact
- [ ] Sounds authentic (not marketing)

## 📝 Top 5 Content Frameworks

### 1. Contrarian Take (5-8% engagement)

```
[Bold contrarian statement]

[Conventional wisdom context]

The issue isn't [surface problem]. It's [root cause].

[Standard practice] was designed for [old context]. 
We're in [new context] now.

The real question: [Reframed problem]?

[Your unique perspective]

What's your experience with [topic]?
```

### 2. Pain Agitation Solution (4-6% engagement)

```
[X]% of [audience] spend [time] on [painful task].

Not because [obvious reason]. Because [root cause].

Late visibility means [consequence]. 
By then, you're [paying the price].

[Time period] of [painful outcome] isn't a natural law. 
It's a systemic failure.

The better way: [Your approach]

The result? [Transformation outcome]

How much time does your team spend on [painful task]?
```

### 3. Personal Story (6-9% engagement)

```
[Time period] ago, I believed [wrong assumption].

I was wrong.

We [struggled with problem]. [Specific example].

The turning point came when [realization].

Here's what we did:
1. [Action 1]
2. [Action 2]
3. [Action 3]

The result: [Transformation outcome]

The lesson: [Universal insight]

Have you experienced [similar situation]?
```

### 4. Technical Deep Dive (3-5% engagement)

```
Most teams think [misconception]. 
Here's the technical reality.

The problem isn't [tool]. The problem is [approach].

Here's how we solved it:
→ [Technical approach 1]
→ [Technical approach 2]
→ [Technical approach 3]

The key: [Integration strategy]. 
It runs on top of [existing systems].

The impact: [Quantified business outcome]

Want to learn more? Drop a comment.
```

### 5. Data-Driven Insights (4-6% engagement)

```
New data: [X]% of [audience] are [doing Y].

Here's why that matters:
[Context and implications]

We analyzed [N] [items] and found:
→ [Insight 1]
→ [Insight 2]
→ [Insight 3]

The actionable takeaway: [Specific recommendation]

What data are you seeing in [area]?
```

## 🚀 Algorithm Optimization

### Critical Ranking Factors

1. **Dwell Time** (40% weight) - Keep readers engaged 30+ seconds
2. **Early Engagement** (30% weight) - First hour is critical
3. **Creator Authority** (15% weight) - Consistent posting, engagement
4. **Relevance** (15% weight) - Hashtags, audience match

### Engagement Hierarchy

- **Comments** = 10 points
- **Shares** = 7 points
- **Saves** = 5 points
- **Clicks** = 3 points
- **Reactions** = 1 point

### Optimal Posting Times

**Best Days**: Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday

**Best Times**:

- 🌅 Morning: 8:00-10:00 AM (peak)
- 🍽️ Lunch: 12:00-1:00 PM (secondary)
- 🌆 Evening: 5:00-6:00 PM (commute)

**Avoid**: Monday mornings, Friday afternoons, weekends

### Formulas

**Engagement Rate** = (Likes + Comments + Shares) / Impressions × 100

**Comment Ratio** = Comments / Total Engagement × 100

**Share Rate** = Shares / Impressions × 100

## 🔧 Troubleshooting

### LinkedIn MCP Issues

**Problem**: "LinkedIn not configured"
**Solution**: Run `npm run setup` in `mcp-servers/linkedin`

**Problem**: "Token expired"
**Solution**: Tokens last 60 days, re-run `npm run setup`

**Problem**: Posts not appearing
**Solution**: Ensure app has "Share on LinkedIn" product enabled

### Content Performance Issues

**Problem**: Low engagement
**Solution**:

- Review first 2 lines hook
- Post at optimal times (Tue-Thu, 8-10 AM)
- Avoid external links in first hour

**Problem**: No comments
**Solution**:

- End with question
- Engage with early commenters
- Reply within 2 hours

**Problem**: Low reach
**Solution**:

- Use native content (no external links initially)
- Increase dwell time (line breaks, formatting)
- Engage with comments to boost algorithm

## 💡 Pro Tips

1. **Hook is Everything** - First 2 lines determine scroll vs. read
2. **Quantify Always** - 40%, 3 months, 10x > "a lot", "some time", "better"
3. **Challenge Assumptions** - "Natural laws" that aren't natural drive debate
4. **Engage Fast** - Reply to comments within 2 hours (algorithm boost)
5. **Native Content** - No external links in first hour (reach penalty)
6. **Line Breaks** - Every 2-3 lines (increases dwell time)
7. **End with Question** - Drives comments (10x more valuable than likes)
8. **Consistency Wins** - 3-5x per week > sporadic posting
