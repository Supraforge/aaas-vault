# Email Clean List Regularly

## When to Use

Use this skill when you need to:
- When managing email lists
- To improve deliverability and reduce costs

## What This Does

Remove inactive subscribers every 3-6 months to maintain list health. Improves deliverability and reduces costs while improving campaign performance.

## Workflow

1. Identify inactive subscribers (no opens/clicks in 3-6 months).
2. Remove inactive subscribers from the email list.
3. Monitor list health and adjust frequency as needed.
4. Implement a process for re-engaging inactive subscribers before removal.
